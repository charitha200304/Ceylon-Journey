Vehicle Rental Information

At Ceylon Journeys, we offer a wide range of vehicles to suit your travel needs in Sri Lanka. Whether you're looking for an economical car for city exploration, a rugged SUV for adventure trips, or a spacious van for group travel, we have the perfect vehicle for you.

All our vehicles are well-maintained, regularly serviced, and come with comprehensive insurance for your peace of mind. Our rental packages include unlimited mileage, 24/7 roadside assistance, and flexible pickup and drop-off options.

Rental Requirements

Valid driver's license, passport, credit card, and minimum age of 21 years. International driving permit recommended for foreign visitors.

Insurance Options

Basic insurance included. Optional comprehensive coverage available for additional protection against damages and theft.

Fuel Policy

All vehicles are provided with a full tank of fuel and should be returned with a full tank. Refueling service available for an additional fee.

Mileage Policy

Unlimited mileage included in all rental packages. Drive as much as you want without worrying about extra charges.

Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need to rent a vehicle?

To rent a vehicle, you'll need a valid driver's license, passport, and a credit card for the security deposit. International visitors should have an International Driving Permit (IDP) along with their national driver's license. The minimum age requirement is 21 years, and drivers under 25 may be subject to a young driver surcharge.

Is insurance included in the rental price?

Yes, basic insurance (Collision Damage Waiver and Theft Protection) is included in the rental price. However, this comes with a deductible amount that you're responsible for in case of damage or theft. We offer additional insurance options to reduce or eliminate the deductible for complete peace of mind.

Can I pick up the vehicle in one location and return it to another?

Yes, we offer one-way rentals between our major locations in Sri Lanka. This service is subject to availability and may incur an additional one-way fee depending on the distance between locations. Please specify your pickup and drop-off locations during the booking process.

What happens if I return the vehicle late?

We provide a grace period of 59 minutes after your scheduled return time. Beyond this grace period, you'll be charged for an additional day of rental. If you know you'll be late, please contact our customer service to extend your rental period and avoid unexpected charges.

Do you offer chauffeur or driver services?

Yes, we offer professional driver services for all our vehicles at an additional daily rate. Our drivers are experienced, knowledgeable about local routes, and speak English. This option is popular for tourists who want to enjoy the scenery without the stress of navigating unfamiliar roads.

Book your vehicle today and enjoy the freedom to discover Sri Lanka at your own pace. Special discounts available for long-term rentals and early bookings.
